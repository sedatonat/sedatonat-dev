About
Sedat Onat

A systems builder by trade. Two decades inside supply chain and purchasing — sourcing, inventory, planning, contracts. The kind of work where every decision compounds and every error has a long tail.

This site is the parallel — a place for software, data, and field notes. Power BI dashboards built for decisions, Swift snippets from the iOS practice, and the writing that ties it all together at TedarikZinciriPortali.com and TZP.news.

The aim is timeless work. Tools, dashboards, and writing that age well — that read as cleanly in five years as they do today.
